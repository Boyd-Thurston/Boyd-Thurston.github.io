A journey to become a web developer

This website is both a record of my journey through the Enspiral Dev Academy's (EDA) foundations curriculum and a live test of my skills as a fledgling web developer.

As I progress through this course it will grow with new articles for both the technical and cultural blogs. The technical blog will showcase new aspects and understandings on my learnings as I progress through the foundation sprints. Whereas the cultural blog will capture my views and reflections as a student and person kicking off with Identity and values as these things normally do.

As always I am one who welcomes constructive criticism and feedback, so don't be afraid to make contact.
