What is my personality type

I found a nice myers briggs personality test on line at 16personalities , I have done similar before however this one is the first to resanate so much with me. My previous attempts with these personality types caused me to question there effectivness with how the discriptions of the results did not fit, this one however feels bang on. It could be that this is a better version however I don't beleave the therory behind them has been updated since developed by Isabel Briggs Myers in the 40's and 50's. The honest reallity is more likely just that honestty. To be effective your answers need to be honest so first you need to be honest with your self, something I have been working on quite a bit lately.

So what was the result? Drum roll please .... INFP-T aka the Mediator

The user friendly terms have changes over time (refered to as the deplomat, the visonary, the dove ect. on other versions) however the lettering is the part that actually comes from the original myers briggs research. The T is an interesting point which I will get to later.

What does it mean

What does it all mean I hear you ask, well that is up for interpritation. So far my research is starting to take form and personally make sense. The key aspect of this personality type is its ability to see things from others prospective, to see the good in everyone and everything, to find hope and try to fix it. Filled with creativity and insperation which is however controdicted as this type of personality also sits on the introverted side of the scale. This controdiction and the rearity of this personality type (the website I used claims it makes up less the 4% of the worlds population) lead to it being miss understood. The information goes further into another controdiction with mediators having a telent for communication and self expression, however with a tendancey to hide away from the world when life goes wrong even to the extent of withdrawing to the point of a hermit (presonally I have lived this controdiction so I can see where this research has come from). Another point which resonated was the visonary rebel with a cause, not challanging the system for the sake of anarchy but for a good reason to make things better, the grater good and all that. They even reach the point where they give themselves so fully to the cause that they forget to focus on day-to-day living (again something that I have lived also). The miss understood nature of this personality type also flows to the perception of group conversations, initily seen as shy or just introverted it is often missed that INFP's generally listen first and intently so while being "shy" for the first bit/couple of times in interactions they are actually collating data, figuring it out and will come out with there point of view when it is time to put it forward and it will be detailed, indepth even leading if they beleave your idea is almost there but not quite. The biggest mistake you can make while dealing with this personality type and my self specificaly is to assume that being quiet means that we are quiet people, quite the oposite we/I will talk your ear off when there is value to add.

That last one is a bit funny actually not in a humorus way more in a realisation of my own habbits kind of way. My self I have always though of my personality is more of a crusaider for the grater good. Not a religous fanatic like the knights templar, more of the same fervor but the belife is in soical justice and equality. I am that guy that always plays the paladin in a D&D game, chooses to help the good NPCs ect. In recent years I became some what political IRL but that didn't go down well with everyone as you can imagine. Queue my lateset withdrawal from the world which comes with an healthy/unhealthy dose of soul searching now to follow what I want for a change, my own cause so to speak. Just a small one, become a web developer and use my expression in a more benifical way.

... that beeing said my mind already wanders ... I see more that could be added ...... web accessability ....... now there is part of the idea that I can still add a bit of equality to my work ......... and that type of thinking is the reason I can not put values out of my mind.

That brings me to the next point taken from the research, and the T from the personality type which stands for turbulent. If you couldn't guess my personality type is governed by their values even ruled by them hence beinging able to lose ones self in a cause. A turbulent Mediator is more likely to lose themselves than an ascertive Mediator. It's an interesting personality querk that can be both a strength and a weakness. We would all agree that being passionate is a good thing however being zealious is not, both come from the same source the difference is the intensity. Something I have known about my self for a long time (which this research supports) is that I need to whatch how much I am letting my self commit to something, its something I need to keep in check and something I have both succeded at and compettly failed at in the past. Just another of lifes greate balancing acts just with a more personal theme.

Making Fun at Behavioural Interviews

This assignment actually asked a couple of STAR question aka a behavoural question. If you have been to a job interview in the last decade you would know the type, so for the fun of it lets pretend this is an interveiw, here goes ...

Interviewer - This first question is about ethics and making ethical decisions. Tell me of a time when you had to make an ethical decison. how did you make your decsion and what was the outcome?

Candidate - In my last role I had to make effical decisions on a dayly bases, mainly around how hard to push collection of debt. On one hand you had the customers that did not have the financial means to do so (or the desire). On the other hand you had clients that need the support that these funds generate and the financail or health needs that not haveing the funds. The ethical decision is who do you help more the customer or the client.

How I dealt with this was to gain more infromation about the customer and their situation (already knowing the importance of the clients situation). Some where having dificulty, some where causing their own difficulty, others should just not be trading at all. Generally you got a feel for how people reasponded to your questioning what is the best course of action.

There where cases when we had to start proceedings to put customers into insolvencey under the idea that constaintly getting further and further into debt was bad for everyone involved not to mention the economy as a whole. There where thankfully more situations that you could work with the customers negotiating on some extra time to allow them to keep trading in the future and keep funding the scheme that benifits the clients.

Interviewer - This next question is about working togeather in a stressful situation. Tell me about a time when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. What strategies you used, how effective where they?

Candidate - In a previous team there were two of us that were considerd subject matter experts (SME's) however we had very differing styles in how we provided information to other team members, so different in fact that we would get frustrated with how the other would explain situations. The competting styles where mine which revlolved around explaining the why and how of a situation so the other could learn they way of thinking so they could resolve issues like this in future. the other individuals styles was based on quick wins, giving the answer only quickly under the idea that no matter how many times the same question gets asked others will eventually get it.

It became apparent that we could not continue under the current situation, so I requested a one on one meeting. We discussed the and the frustrations. We also discussed the merits of the qucik win and the educating approach our working environment. We came to an agreement that we would split the work based upon need.

The outcome was if the team member needed quick information to get strait back to the customer say if they had been waiting a while or had to go through multiple request to hold while the team member asked different questions from us then the qucik win approach would be used. However if the situation had not been resolved in multiple calls, or the team member had ask the same question more than one then the education approach would be taken.

Interviewer - If you were in that situation again, would you do any thing different?

Candidate - Ask for the one on one meeting sooner, planing the course of action made everything run smoother from then on.
